BTS

What does BTS stand for?

also known as Bangtan Boys, is an acronym for the Korean expression Bangtan Sonyeondan, meaning Bulletproof Boy Scouts

When did they start?

The seven member band was formed through auditions by Big Hit Entertainment in 2010 and 2011, and finalized in 2012. BTS made their debut in 2013 with the first of their school trilogy series, their single album, 2 Cool 4 Skool. The group quickly became one of the hottest acts in South Korea, winning several New Artist of the Year awards.

What happend next?

BTS evolved their identity, channeling different themes throughout their music, through eras like School, The Most Beautiful Moment in Life and Wings. In just 4 years, the band has completed three world tours, released four studio albums (two Korean and two Japanese) and five EPs, gaining millions of fans across the world -- though it was their 2017 Billboard Awards win for Top Social Artist that marked the start of their breakthrough in the U.S.

What is Love Yourself: Her?

Love Yourself:Her is BTS latest EP and the next chapter of their career. The album was pre-ordered over 1 million times over two weeks before its release, but the records don't stop there. Love Yourself had already topped the iTunes Album charts at No. 1 in a record-breaking 73 countries, the largest debut release for any South Korean artist. And "DNA" the album's lead sigle, is also blowing up on Youtube with nearly 30 million views since it's early release
